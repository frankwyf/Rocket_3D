# How to Play SkyRocket 3D

## Before You Start
When the game opens, a **Start Menu** appears first.

1. Choose a mode by clicking one of the three mode buttons:
   - **Campaign**
   - **Time Attack**
   - **Collector**
2. Click **START** to begin.

You can also use keyboard:
- `1` = Campaign
- `2` = Time Attack
- `3` = Collector
- `Enter` = Start

## Modes

### 1) Campaign (Progressive Objectives)
- Round 1: Launch and land on the far pad
- Round 2: Toggle split view (`V`) during flight, then land
- Round 3: Change camera mode (`C`) during flight, then land

### 2) Time Attack
- 5 short rounds
- Land quickly
- Collect at least **1 bonus ring** before landing

### 3) Collector
- 3 rounds
- Collect **all 3 bonus rings** before landing

## Core Controls
- `W/A/S/D`: Camera movement
- `Q/E`: Vertical camera move
- `Mouse`: Look around
- `Shift`: Faster camera move
- `Ctrl`: Slower camera move
- `F`: Launch rocket
- `V`: Split-view toggle
- `C`: Camera mode switch
- `R`: Reset current campaign run
- `H`: Toggle help text (window title)
- `Esc`: Exit

## On-screen Buttons
- Red button (`L`): Launch
- Green button (`R`): Reset

## Scoring
- Round clear gives base points + time bonus
- Bonus rings add extra score
- Failed objectives reduce score and retry the round
