# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue with the title prefix `conduct:`.
